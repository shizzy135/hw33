About the Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics are one of the most legendary and successful franchises in NBA history, founded in 1946. Based in Boston, Massachusetts, the Celtics compete in the Eastern Conference's Atlantic Division. The Celtics are famous for their iconic green and white colors, symbolizing pride and tradition. Throughout the decades, the Celtics have built a reputation for excellence, teamwork, and relentless competitive spirit. The Celtics' passionate fanbase, strong team culture, and rich heritage have made the team beloved worldwide. Legends such as Bill Russell, Larry Bird, Paul Pierce, and John Havlicek have proudly worn the Celtics jersey, solidifying the team's historic legacy. Boston Celtics games, held at TD Garden, offer fans unforgettable experiences filled with excitement, pride, and community spirit.
